Who We Are

A Joint Venture Built on Expertise

Synergy Air LLC is a California-registered aviation logistics and aircraft solutions company formed as a strategic joint venture between two established companies — combining U.S. commercial capability with Central Asian operational aviation expertise.

"Together, the partnership combines international commercial capability with operational aviation expertise — bridging North America and Central Asia."
SkyLights V Inc.
U.S.-Based Commercial Partner
U.S.-based commercial operations partner providing business development, logistics coordination, regulatory navigation, and client acquisition across North American and international markets.
KAP KG Aircompany LLC
Central Asia Aviation Operations Partner
Central Asia–based aviation operator contributing direct fleet access, regional market expertise, and proven operational infrastructure across Kyrgyzstan and adjacent corridors.

Market Opportunity

A High-Growth Global Corridor

Global air cargo demand is expanding steadily, driven by e-commerce growth, supply chain diversification, and increasing cross-border trade.

North America → Central Asia
An underserved, high-value connectivity corridor with limited direct competition and growing bilateral trade volume between the U.S. and Central Asian markets.
Flexible Charter Demand
Rising need for on-demand, non-scheduled cargo and passenger charter services across emerging market corridors as global supply chains diversify.
Emerging Market Access
Kyrgyzstan, Kazakhstan, and adjacent regional markets represent underserved hubs connecting Europe, the Middle East, and Asia with strong growth trajectories.
